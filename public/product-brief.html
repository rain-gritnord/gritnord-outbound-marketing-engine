The Gritnord Platform

End-to-end AI-powered B2B meeting engine.

What is Gritnord

How it works
1

UC Onboarding

The Unique Customer (UC) defines their Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) — industry, company size, pain points, geography. Claude extracts and locks this into a structured JSON used by all downstream systems.

2

DC Research Pipeline

The ICP is sent to Clay via webhook. Apollo finds 250 matching Dream Customer (DC) companies per batch. BetterContact enriches with email and mobile. Claude scores each 0–100 against the ICP.

3

Content Engine generates awareness

30+ RSS feeds curated daily. Claude generates LinkedIn posts in Rain's voice with 8 learned guidelines. Auto-posted via LinkedIn API. Engagement tracked and fed back to the learning engine.

4

30-Day 8-Channel Outreach Sequence

Each DC enters a coordinated 8-touch sequence: paid social → LinkedIn DM → HeyGen video → BDR call → voice note → WhatsApp → BDR call 2 → breakup message. Every touch references the last.

5

Meeting Booking & Gate

DCs who respond book via Cal.com with an ICP-gate qualification form. Routing assigns the correct UC. Confirmation + reminder sequences fire automatically.

6

No-Show Prevention

T-24h and T-2h reminders via email + WhatsApp. If DC doesn't join at T+3min, ElevenLabs places an AI voice call and offers 3 reschedule slots via Make.com.

7

Billing & Flywheel

Admin marks meeting held → Stripe fires €500 charge. 48h later, the DC receives a pitch: "Want meetings like this?" — converting DC to UC at zero acquisition cost. The flywheel spins.
