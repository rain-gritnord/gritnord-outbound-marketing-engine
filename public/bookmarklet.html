LinkedIn Engagement Bookmarklet

One click on any LinkedIn post you've published — engagement syncs to Gritnord automatically. No typing.

1

Drag the button below into your bookmarks bar

Make sure your bookmarks bar is visible (⌘+Shift+B in Chrome / Safari).

Drag this button to your bookmarks bar ↓

⚡ Gritnord Stats
2

Open the analytics page for any post

Go to your LinkedIn profile or feed → click the impression count under any post you've published → it opens the analytics summary page. URL looks like:
linkedin.com/analytics/post-summary/urn:li:activity:XXXXX/

This page shows Impressions, Reactions, Comments, Reposts and Followers gained — all the data Gritnord needs.

3

Click the bookmarklet

A small popup appears from localhost:3000 showing the captured score. It closes automatically in 3 seconds. Done.

What it reads: reactions count, comments count, reposts count — already loaded in your browser.
What it sends: those 3 numbers + the post URN → localhost:3000
What Gritnord does: converts to a score, feeds the AI learner, shows in Performance tab.

Safe for your LinkedIn account

  • Makes zero requests to LinkedIn servers — reads only what your browser already loaded
  • Only activates on linkedin.com/feed/update/ pages — nowhere else
  • LinkedIn sees a normal human visiting a post — because that's exactly what's happening
  • No credentials, no tokens, no LinkedIn API calls
  • Data goes only to localhost:3000 — your own machine
