Product Flow

From your ideal customer definition to qualified meetings in your calendar — fully managed, no retainer risk.

Step 01
You define your ideal customer
Industry, company size, role, geography. Your best three existing customers become the anchor. We extract what makes them a perfect fit and turn that into a precise targeting profile.
Takes 30 minutes
Step 02
We build your target list
100 qualified dream customer contacts with 2 or more decision-maker personas per target. Ranked by fit, mapped to their channels, events, and entry points. Ready within 48 hours.
48 hours
Step 03
Personalised outreach starts
LinkedIn, WhatsApp, email, cold calling — every channel your dream customer actually uses. Content marketing and SEO run in parallel to build inbound authority while outbound works the list.
Step 04
Meetings land in your calendar
5 qualified dream customer meetings per month, managed end-to-end. Reminders, check-ins, no-show recovery, and instant rebook — so you show up to conversations, not coordination.
5 meetings / month
Step 05
Every meeting billed — no retainer risk
€500 per meeting held. Monthly consolidated invoice. No meeting held means no charge for that slot. The incentive is entirely aligned with your outcome.

Step 06
Your contacts become tomorrow's pipeline
Post-meeting nurture turns dream customer prospects into referrers and new customers. The network grows with every cycle. The cost per meeting drops as the system learns.
